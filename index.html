Long Chen

I work on AI and computational imaging, with a particular focus on medical imaging and underwater imaging. My research explores efficient, robust, and intelligent visual perception models for challenging imaging scenarios.

Bio

I am a researcher at Imperial College London, supported by Prof. Declan O’Regan and Prof. Wenjia Bai. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at University College London, and I received my PhD from the University of Leicester. My research interests lie in AI and computational imaging, with a particular focus on medical imaging and underwater imaging. I also co-lead two research groups: the Brain-Inspired Computing Group, co-lead with Prof. Qi Xu at Dalian University of Technology since 2023, and the Underwater Imaging Group, co-lead with Prof. Xiaopeng Liu at Shandong University of Science and Technology since 2023.
