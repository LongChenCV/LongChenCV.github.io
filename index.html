Long Chen

I work on AI and computational imaging, with a particular focus on medical imaging and underwater imaging. My research explores efficient, robust, and intelligent visual perception models for challenging imaging scenarios.

Bio

I am a researcher at Imperial College London, supported by Prof. Declan O’Regan and Prof. Wenjia Bai. Previously, I was a Research Fellow at University College London supported by Prof. Matt Clarkson and Dr Thomas Dowrick. I received my PhD from the University of Leicester, supervised by Prof. Huiyu Zhou and Prof. Junyu Dong. My research interests lie in AI and computational imaging, with a particular focus on medical imaging and underwater imaging.

Brain-Inspired Computing Group
